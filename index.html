About Us

A Local Builder You Can Actually Talk To

MPH Builders started in 2004 with two blokes, a ute and a commitment to doing things right. Over twenty years later, Mick Hernyk and his team of 11 — plus a network of trusted local subcontractors — are still building across every corner of Tasmania.

We're not a volume builder churning out cookie-cutter homes. We're the kind of builders who answer the phone, explain what's happening, and make sure you're happy with the result. That's how we've kept going for two decades — by treating every job like our name's on it. Because it is.

In 2013, Mick was awarded the Master Builders Tasmania Young Builder of the Year — a recognition of the quality and professionalism that goes into every project we take on.
